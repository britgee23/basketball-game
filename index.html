How to Play Three-Point-Shootout


Choose your difficulty setting: Rookie is the easiest and All-Star is the most challenging.
Start the Shot Clock by clicking on the basketball.
Click and hold the basketball to begin shooting.
Release the click when the meter fills up green and says "Shoot!"
A message will appear in the upper right corner giving you feedback on your shot.
The ball will reappear in a random location along the shooting line.
Each made shot is worth 1 point, except the "Money Ball" which is worth 2 points.
Score the most amount of points within the time limit to win!
